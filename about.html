How I got started ?

After graduating, I began my job search for data analyst or data scientist positions. While browsing the internet, I stumbled upon some stunning websites that sparked a newfound passion for web development. This fascination led me to explore online courses and bootcamps, but none truly resonated with me until I discovered Scrimba. Their approach perfectly aligned with my learning style, and I'm thrilled to have embarked on this journey as a future front-end developer.

How I stay committed to learning ?

Well, I've always imagined myself finally becoming a front-end developer, ending projects, and making clients satisfied. This fuels my passion, the dream of creating something real and seeing the positive impact on others. And I believe that consistency and small steps are what make the learning journey easy and fun. Of course, I always focus on the success of how much I've progressed, not how far I still must go. This mindset keeps me motivated and excited to keep learning.
